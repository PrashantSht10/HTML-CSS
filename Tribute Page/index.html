Jurgen Klopp

The man who changed doubters to believers

Here's Jurgen's timeline at Liverpool:

  • 2015: Joined Liverpool FC
  • 2018–19: Won club's sixth UEFA Champions League
  • 2019: Won club's fourth UEFA Super Cup and first FIFA Club World Cup in club's history
  • 2019–20: Won club's first Premier League title in 30 years
  • 2021–22: Won domestic double including FA Cup and Carabao Cup
  • 2023–24: Won league cup for the record tenth time
  • 2024: Leaves Liverpool FC
